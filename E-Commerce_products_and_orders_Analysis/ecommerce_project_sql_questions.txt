 DATABASE & BASIC RETRIEVAL QUESTIONS
1. Create a database named project and switch to it.
2. Display all tables present in the project database.
3. Retrieve all records from the ecommerce_customers table.
4. Retrieve all records from the ecommerce_orders table.
5. Fetch all orders where the region is South and the price is greater than 500.
6. Find the total number of orders available in the ecommerce_orders table.

 AGGREGATE FUNCTION QUESTIONS
7. Calculate the total revenue using price × quantity from all orders.
8. Find the average order value using price × quantity.
9. Calculate total revenue for each region.
10. Find the total number of items sold (quantity) in each region and sort the result by total items sold.

  ORDER BY & LIMIT QUESTIONS
11. Retrieve the first 5 orders sorted by price in ascending order, showing order ID, customer name, and price.

 JOIN-BASED QUESTIONS
12. Display order ID, customer name, email, city, and price by joining ecommerce_customers and ecommerce_orders tables.
13. Calculate total revenue generated by each city using a join between customers and orders tables.

 CONDITIONAL (CASE) QUESTIONS
14. Classify orders into price categories (premium, midlevel, budget) based on price and display category-wise sorted results.

 SUBQUERY QUESTIONS
15. Retrieve all orders where the price is greater than the average price of all orders.

  DATE & TIME QUESTIONS
16. Calculate monthly total revenue using order date (formatted as year-month).

 PRODUCT & CATEGORY ANALYSIS QUESTIONS
17. Find the top 5 best-selling products based on total quantity sold.
18. Calculate total revenue generated for each product category and sort by revenue.
